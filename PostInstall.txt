== TO USE:

Require RspecSpinner on spec/spec_helper.rb:

  require 'spec'
  require 'rspec_spinner'
  ...
  

Change your spec.opts --formatter to "spinner":

  --formatter RspecSpinner::Bar
  
or:

  --formatter RspecSpinner::Spinner
                            
                            
Have fun!

http://github.com/nofxx/rspec_spinner
